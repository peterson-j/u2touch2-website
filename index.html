The Brain Behind the Bite

Dr. R’s AI engine brings precision diagnosis into the first mile of care — without relying on costly, radiation‑based diagnostics. Our platform is built to work from a simple selfie or video, captured on a smartphone or intra‑oral camera, making early detection accessible to anyone, anywhere.

Our system doesn’t just detect anomalies — it models how issues may progress over time, estimates future treatment complexity and cost, and helps users act early. As the platform learns, our proprietary “Oral Quotient Score” quantifies how orally fit a person is in relation to their overall health — giving individuals and care providers a clear, actionable metric.

We’re also building a voice‑based conversational layer to democratize access even further, especially for non‑literate or non‑digital users. This isn’t just an AI tool — it’s an intelligent bridge between oral neglect and oral action, built for scale, grounded in data, and designed to compound advantage over time.
