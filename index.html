Engineering the Future of Dental Care

U2 Touch2 Solutions LLP is a deep-tech venture reimagining oral healthcare at scale. We are using AI, diagnostics, mathematical modeling and optimzation and are designing intelligent tools that detect oral anomalies early, model disease progression, and guide patients to timely, affordable treatment — from first image to final intervention.

The problem is massive.
90% of Indians have underlying oral issues — but don’t know it.
And when they do, 70% delay treatment due to high cost.

This isn’t just a health gap — it’s an insight gap.
We help users understand what’s happening in their mouths, what could happen if left untreated, and what action they can take — all from a simple image taken on a mobile phone or intraoral camera.

We believe early oral intervention is the next frontier in preventive care. And we’re building the rails for it — today.
